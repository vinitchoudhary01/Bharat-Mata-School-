Bharat Mata School

Bharat Mata School is one of the best RBSE schools in Rajasthan, sprawled over in 100 bighas of land with modern architecture and wide space. The location, climate and infrastructure are very conducive and eco-friendly for a healthy academic life. It is located on main road (Udaipur-Nawalgarh bypass) of the nawalgarh city, free from the noise and pollution of the city in an extremely peaceful area. Our endeavour is to equip Bharat Mata School with the best teaching faculty & best facilities found at an international school. Bharat Mata School was envisaged as co-educational RBSE Hindi medium school with a new approach, established by BHARAT MATA SCHOOL. It is a purpose built school, it has learning spaces to support the child and respect his/her expression of individuality.
